nidaba¶

nidaba is an open source distributed optical character recognition pipeline that makes it easy to preprocess and OCR scans of text documents in a multitude of ways.

nidaba is a powerful tool allowing you to mix and combine some of the most advanced free image processing and character recognition software and distribute the execution of this software to multiple machines. While the learning curve may be high at first, you’ll be able in no time to process your own scanned documents.

  • Binarization
